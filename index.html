About Us

ZAHABU BRAND

Francis Zahabu is a Rwandan fashion brand founded by designer Francis Iraguha. The brand is known for its high-quality, custom-made clothing that blends traditional African prints with modern tailoring, Francis Zahabu has been recognized for pioneering the"Made in Rwanda" fashion movement, emphasizing local craftsmanship and cultural pride.
